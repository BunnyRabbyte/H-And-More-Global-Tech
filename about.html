About H & More

A detail-obsessed team focused on reliable, future-ready installs.

H & More Global Tech is a specialist electrical and security systems company headquartered in Wuse, Abuja. We combine clean installation practices with robust engineering so that every system we deploy looks good today and still works perfectly years from now.

Our work spans residential homes, office complexes, hotels, estates and specialised facilities. We understand the realities of the Nigerian power grid, and design solutions that stay stable even under rough conditions.

From the first site survey to full hand-over, our team handles design, installation, testing and user training in-house. Whether it’s a compact smart home, a new office fit-out or a multi-building estate, we approach each project with the same precision.

Our edge is simple: honest load calculations, quality materials and disciplined workmanship. No shortcuts, no guess-work — just infrastructure you can trust.
